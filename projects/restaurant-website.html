Case Study

Restaurant Website

A mobile-first business website focused on making menus, hours, reservations, and contact information easy to find on any screen.

Project Overview

This project was designed as a modern restaurant website concept for a local business that needed a cleaner visual identity and a much better mobile experience.

The Problem

Many restaurant websites bury their most important information. Menus are hard to find, hours are unclear, and mobile users have to hunt for basic contact details. That creates friction for customers and makes the business feel dated.

The Solution

The redesign focused on speed, clarity, and strong hierarchy. Important actions like viewing the menu, checking hours, and making contact were brought to the front. The visual design was simplified so the brand felt more modern and more trustworthy.

Technology Used

  • Semantic HTML5
  • Modern CSS layout
  • Responsive design
  • JavaScript enhancements
  • Performance-first structure

Results

The finished concept creates a much smoother mobile experience, clearer business messaging, and a stronger foundation for local SEO and conversions.
